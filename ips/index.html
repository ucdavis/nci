National Core Indicators In-Person Survey for the State of California.

“The NCI In-Person Survey is administered to individuals with a developmental disability who receive at least one service other than case management.”
(National Child Family Survey Report, NASDDDS & HSRI)

For optimal performance, please use supported browsers Edge, Chrome, Firefox, or Safari with the most recent updates installed.

To get started, choose a survey section and topic, and make selections for:

  1. Survey question
  2. Regional center
  3. Year

Year Optional filters are available to explore responses based on demographic characteristics. Regional center can also be selected by clicking on the map. Charts will automatically update.

Export views of the data as an image or a PDF by selecting the corresponding download button in the lower right corner of the dashboard view.
